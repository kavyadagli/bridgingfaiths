Purpose

As our communities grow increasingly diverse, it is important that we can learn to accept and celebrate our differences. In order to accept our differences, we must first understand them.

Unfortunately, in today's world the media is often a source of generalization and miscommunication. Even though they media is unreliable in capturing the entire picture, it continues to be the main source of information for many. I began the project "Bridging Faiths" because I was curious as to how much of this information was accurate. After watching and reading the interviews with religious leaders on this site I hope you are able to find beauty in these religions and learn a bit more about them.

Process

When I first began this project my first instinct was to turn to the internet to begin my research. Then I thought that this might be counter intuitive because the internet is many times the source of misinformation. My solution was to contact religious leaders in the hopes that they could share their perspective on what the core beliefs of their religions are and how they are conforming to modern times.

Findings

After completing many interviews, I found that at their core, religions are focused on devotion, a strong sense of community, and giving to others.
